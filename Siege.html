Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is an online tactical shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. From the tactical gameplay and unique operators to fun with your friends as you attempt to infiltrate the base of the "defenders" or fend of the attackers as they try to breach in and complete their objective. This game tackles a new feel in tactical gaming that has you working up strategies with your teammates as you attempt to defeat your opponents.
