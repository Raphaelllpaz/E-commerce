The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Price:$39.99

Raphy's Rating

Initial Release date: November 11, 2011

Raphy's Rating

Developed and published by Bethesda studios, the fifth installment in the Elder Scrolls series is nothing more then a masterpiece and Game of the year winner, The game's main story revolves around the player's character, the Dragonborn, on their quest to defeat Alduin the World-Eater, a dragon who is prophesied to destroy the world. The game is set 200 years after the events of Oblivion and takes place in Skyrim, the northernmost province of Tamriel. Over the course of the game, the player completes quests and develops the character by improving skills.

Chances are if you have never heard of Skyrim you have probably been living under a rock.
