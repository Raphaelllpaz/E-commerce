Fortnite

Price:Free! (In-game Purchases)

Initial Release date: July 21, 2017

Raphy's Rating

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games. Fortnite took the world by storm when it released its battle royal mode, the game began to catch the attention of people around the world as people started to play the game in the competitive field and tournaments were held where winners earned millons of dollars. In my opinion Fortnite changed the way the gaming world is to this day, it revolutionized the title of "gamer" with many making big money from streaming on services such as "twitch". The game has started to have a steep drop as people have now gotten bored but the fan base is still imense and as long as they keep updating the game, Fortnite will never completely die out.
